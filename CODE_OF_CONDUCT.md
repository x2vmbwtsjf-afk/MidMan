# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in the `midman` community should be respectful, constructive, and professional.

We expect contributors to:

- communicate with respect
- give and receive feedback constructively
- assume good intent while discussing technical disagreements
- help make the project welcoming to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- repeated disruptive behavior in issues, discussions, or pull requests
- publishing private information without permission

## Collaboration Expectations

Technical disagreement is normal in an open-source project. What matters is how it is handled.

When reviewing or discussing work:

- focus on the code, design, or behavior rather than the person
- explain concerns clearly and specifically
- stay open to correction
- keep discussion grounded in facts, safety, and maintainability

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, request changes in behavior, or remove contributions that violate this policy.

The goal of enforcement is to protect a healthy working environment for the community.
